# Enable AI Features

Connect your preferred AI provider for intelligent analysis:

| Provider | Key Required | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **OpenAI** | Yes (`sk-...`) | GPT-4o, fast & reliable |
| **Anthropic** | Yes (`sk-ant-...`) | Claude, deep understanding |
| **Azure OpenAI** | Yes | Enterprise, private deployments |
| **Ollama** | No (local) | Privacy-first, offline use |
| **DeepSeek** | Yes | Cost-effective, high quality |

**Enterprise features:** mTLS certificates, custom CA bundles, PFX/P12 support.

AI features are **fully optional** — the extension works 100% offline without them.
