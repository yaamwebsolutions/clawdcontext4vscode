# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want ClawdContext to be a high-signal, respectful engineering community.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and direct
- critique ideas, not people
- provide evidence when making technical claims
- assume good intent, then clarify disagreements quickly
- keep discussions on-topic and actionable

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insulting language
- repeated bad-faith arguing or derailment
- publishing private information without permission
- spam, scams, or malicious content

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit/remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact the maintainers through the support/security channels in `SUPPORT.md` and `SECURITY.md`.
